Regular Meeting

The monthly regular meeting takes place on every third Monday of the month. It is an opportunity for the PhDs of the graduate school to present their research and discuss it with our multidisciplinary team. Besides that, it is a great platform for networking and exchange.

Training Courses

Our graduate school offers regularly high-quality soft-skill training courses to its members. The courses are designed to support the personal and professional development of the PhDs and to prepare them for their future careers.
