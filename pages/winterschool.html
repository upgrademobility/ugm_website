About the Winter School

The UpGrade Mobility Graduate School Winter School is the annual networking event of the KIT Mobility Systems Center. Within one week, PhD students from a wide range of mobility disciplines come together to learn, get to know each other, and engage in interdisciplinary training.

Network
Connect with PhD students from the Graduate School, other participants, and KIT professors.
Expertise
Learn new ideas and deepen your knowledge through activities with other participants and KIT professors.
Karlsruhe
Discover the south of Germany while enjoying activities and workshops together in the city of Karlsruhe.

Application Process

If you want to apply for the UpGrade Mobility Winter School, please attach the following information to your application:

  • Short CV including contact details
  • Confirmation of your supervisor
  • Name of your home university
  • (Working) title of your PhD thesis

Currently the application process is closed.
