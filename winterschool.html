About the Winter School

The UpGrade Mobility Graduate School Winter School is the annual networking event of the KIT Mobility Systems Center. Within one week, PhD students from a wide range of mobility disciplines come together to learn, get to know each other, and engage in interdisciplinary training.

Network
Connect with PhD students from the graduate school, other participants, and KIT professors.
Expertise
Learn new ideas and deepen your knowledge in lectures by participants and KIT professors.
Karlsruhe
Discover the south of Germany while enjoying activities and meals together in the city of Karlsruhe.

Upcoming 2023 Edition

For the Winter School, 15 places are available for guests, for which every doctoral student from the subject area of mobility can register. A direct registration procedure with a waiting list will be used. The closing date for applications is 23 October 2022. The Graduate School will cover costs for accommodation and meals during the Winter School. Each participant must finance travel to the conference venue in Karlsruhe on their own.

Application Process

If you are curious and want to apply for the UpGrade Mobility Winter School, please attach the following information to your application:

  • Short CV including contact details
  • Confirmation of your supervisor
  • Name of your home university
  • (Working) title of your PhD thesis
